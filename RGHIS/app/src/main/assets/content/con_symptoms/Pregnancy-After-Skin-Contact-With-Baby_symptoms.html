Benefits

  • Happier Baby
    Skin-to-skin contact immediately after birth keeps the baby warm and calm. The baby cries less and has a more stable heart rate, breathing pattern, blood oxygen and sugar levels.
  • Enhanced Bonding
    In research studies, mothers with skin-to-skin contact with their babies have demonstrated better bonding with increased affection and attachment behaviour.
  • Better Breastfeeding
    Skin-to-skin contact promotes breastfeeding immediately after birth. It takes advantage of the babys early alertness and innate behaviour to latch on to the breast, often without assistance.
