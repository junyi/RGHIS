How do I know if I have Thyroid Eye Disease?

Patients with Thyroid Eye Disease may complain of the following:

A 'staring' appearance
Protrusion of the eye
Tearing
Eye discomfort and gritty sensation
Eye redness
Puffy eyelids
Double vision
Squint
Decreased vision
