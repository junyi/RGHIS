Symptoms


Common Symptoms

  • Recent memory loss that affects job performance
  • Difficulty performing familiar tasks
  • Problems with language
  • Disorientation with time and space
  • Poor or decreased judgement
  • Problems with abstract thinking
  • Misplacing things
  • Changes in personality
  • Loss of initiative
  • Changes in mood or behaviour

(Adapted from 'Someone to Stand by You': A guide for caregivers affected by Alzheimers disease or a related dementia. Alzheimers Disease Association: Los Angeles, Riverside and San Bernardino Counties.)
