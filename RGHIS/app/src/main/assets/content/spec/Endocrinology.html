Endocrinology is the field of medicine dealing with the disorder of the endocrine system (system of glands) and its specific secretions called hormones. The endocrine system is instrumental in regulating mood, growth and development, tissue function and metabolism, as well as sexual function and reproductive processes.

Under the SingHealth group, the specialty is offered by Singapore General Hospital (SGH) and KK Women's and Children's Hospital (KKH). SGH provides tertiary level care in hormonal and metabolic disorders; and KKH provides comprehensive cover for children with congenital and acquired conditions.
