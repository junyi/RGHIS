Medical oncology is the field of medicine that deals with the treatment of cancer primarily with drugs, for example, chemotherapy. The specialty is related to surgical oncology and radiation oncology.

National Cancer Centre Singapore offers inpatient and outpatient clinical services focusing on Breast Cancer, Lung Cancer, Head & Neck Cancer, Gastrointestinal Cancer, Gynaecologic & Genitourinary Cancer and Lymphoma. KK Women's and Children's Hospital offers a range of services in haematology and oncology, focusing specifically on children.
