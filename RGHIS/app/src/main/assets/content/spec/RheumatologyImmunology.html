Rheumatology & Immunology is the specialty that deals with the study and treatment of rheumatic diseases and conditions and functions of the immune system.

Singapore General Hospital (SGH) provides clinical services for the diagnosis and management of all rheumatology-related conditions. Clinical services include General Rheumatology and Inpatient management, including use of biologic agents in severe arthritis. KK Women's & Children's Hospital (KKH) evaluates children with limb and joint pains providing complete care for the children.
