Skin-to-Skin Contact With Your Baby

What is skin-to-skin?

  • Skin-to-skin means placing the naked newborn baby onto the mothers chest.
  • The nurse dries the baby, places him onto the mothers chest and covers the babys back with a warm blanket.
  • Both the mother and the baby should be kept in this position for at least the first hour of life, as much as possible.
  • Routine newborn care practices for the baby can be performed while the baby is still on the mothers chest.
