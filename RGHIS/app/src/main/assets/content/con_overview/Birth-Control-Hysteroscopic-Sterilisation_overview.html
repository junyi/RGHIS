Hysteroscopic Sterilisation

Hysteroscopic sterilisation is a new method for permanent birth control. Like other forms of birth control, hysteroscopic sterilisation has its benefits and risks that need to be considered.

Some of the benefits of hysteroscopic sterilisation are :

  • No hospital stay - Hysteroscopic sterilisation is done as an outpatient procedure in the Day Surgery. There is no need to be admitted to hospital.
  • No anaesthesia risks - Hysteroscopic sterilisation can be done under local anaesthesia or even without anaesthesia. Hence the risks of general anaesthesia are avoided.
  • No incision wound or scarring - Hysteroscopic sterilisation is done through the normal birth passage. Hence there is no surgical cut required.
  • Quick - The procedure takes only about 30 minutes.
  • Fast recovery - Following the procedure, you should be able to return to your daily routine activities in a day or two.
