What treatments are there for anophthalmic socket?

Depending on the problem, the following treatment may be prescribed:

Anti-allergy eyedrops
Surgery to increase the orbital volume and/or reconstruction of the fornices.
Examples: secondary orbital implants or mucous membrane grafting

At SNEC, we have a dedicated clinic that specializes in custom-made prosthesis eyes for our patients to improve their fit and cosmetic appearance. More challenging cases may require several operations to achieve satisfactory results.
