What kinds of treatments are available for Age-Related Macular Degeneration?

  • Laser photocoagulation: A surgical procedure involving the application of a hot laser
  • Photo Dynamic Therapy: This method uses a non-thermal laser together with an intravenous drug to seal and halt or slow the progression of the condition
  • Drugs: Macugen and Lucentis are two drugs delivered to the eye through injections.

Your eye doctor will be able to advise you on the best course of treatment for your AMD.
